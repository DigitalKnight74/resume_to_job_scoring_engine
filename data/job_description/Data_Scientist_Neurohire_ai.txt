About

NeuroHire is building an AI-first SaaS platform that helps companies make smarter hiring decisions using real data — not guesswork. We work with complex, messy hiring datasets and turn them into insights, models, and product features that directly impact outcomes.

We’re looking for a Data Scientist who enjoys going deep into data, building models, and influencing product decisions — not just running analyses.

If you like combining statistics, machine learning, and business thinking to solve real problems, you’ll fit right in.

What You’ll Work On

At NeuroHire, Data Scientists sit at the intersection of data, product, and AI.

Analyze large datasets to uncover patterns, trends, and insights

Build predictive models for matching, ranking, and hiring outcomes

Work on NLP problems like resume understanding, skill extraction, and candidate-job alignment

Design experiments and evaluate feature performance (A/B testing)

Collaborate with product and engineering teams to translate insights into features

Define and track key metrics to measure model and product performance

Clean, process, and structure messy real-world datasets

Communicate findings clearly to both technical and business stakeholders

Continuously improve models using real-world feedback

What We’re Looking For

We value strong fundamentals and practical impact.

2–5+ years of experience in data science or applied ML roles

Strong foundation in statistics, probability, and machine learning

Proficiency in Python (pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, etc.)

Experience building and evaluating predictive models

Familiarity with NLP techniques or unstructured data

Experience working with SQL and large datasets

Strong analytical thinking and problem-solving skills

Ability to communicate insights clearly and effectively

Comfortable working in a fast-paced startup environment

Nice to Have (Not Required)

Experience with deep learning or transformer-based models

Exposure to LLMs or generative AI applications

Experience with experimentation frameworks (A/B testing)

Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure)

Experience in SaaS or product-driven companies
